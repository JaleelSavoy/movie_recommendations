MoviEvolve: Find The Right Movie for You!

Model-based, User-based Collaborative Filtering for Film Recommendations

Methodology

  • Built a model-based, user-based collaborative filtering model that recommends movies based on the cluster group a user falls into
  • Hierarchical agglomerative clustering technique was used to establish four clusters of users (labeled 0 to 3)
  • Data was collected from the GroupLens Research Group
  • Relevant Paper: F. Maxwell Harper and Joseph A. Konstan. 2015. The MovieLens Datasets: History and Context. ACM Transactions on Interactive Intelligent Systems (TiiS) 5, 4: 19:1â€“19:19
  • The data were created by 610 users between March 29, 1996 and September 24, 2018
  • This dataset was generated on September 26, 2018
